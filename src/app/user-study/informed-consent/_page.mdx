## Goal of the research

The aim of our research is to investigate data visualization techniques used in players metrics.
We aim to understand how players use visualizations of gameplay data in Metroidvania games. If they find a benefit in using them and what is important to them.

Your participation in this study will contribute to the advancement of knowledge on player facing data visualization techniques,
and is very welcome.

## Informed Consent Form: Please read carefully.
