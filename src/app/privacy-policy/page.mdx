# HKViz Privacy Policy

HKViz is a web application and mod for the game HollowKnight and is mainly developed by Oliver Gstöttenbauer ("we", "us", "our") as part of his bachelor thesis conducted at the [Institute of Computer Graphics, Johannes Kepler University](https://www.jku.at/en/institute-of-computer-graphics/).
It is not affiliated with [TeamCherry](https://www.teamcherry.com.au/), the developer of HollowKnight, in any way. This privacy policy explains what data is collected by HKViz and how it is used.

## Contents

## What data do we collect?

**Personal identification information:**

-   email address
-   usernames (discord, google, ...)
-   ip address
-   device names, where the mod is used on (e.g. "My PC")

**Timestamped HollowKnight gameplay data / gameplay events (If you are using the mod)**

-   Player location
-   Abilities used
-   Health and soul
-   Almost all data contained in the save file over time like
    -   number of soul vessels
    -   collected charms
    -   geo
    -   essence
    -   ...

## How do we collect your data?

You directly provide us with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

-   Register for our web application or inside the HKViz mod.
-   Complete a survey or provide feedback on any of our message boards or via email.
-   Use or view our website via your browser's cookies.
-   Use the HKViz mod to upload your gameplay data.

We may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

-   Discord (if you use the discord login)
-   Google (if you use the google login)

## How will we use your data?

We collect your data so that we can:

-   Identify you as a user of our web application or mod.
-   Identify your gameplay data as belonging to you, so that you can view it in the web application and manage its visibility.
-   Email you with login links and other information about the web application, research projcet or mod.
-   Allow you to analyze your gameplay data in the web application and/or share it with other users.
-   Allow you to view and analyze gameplay data of other users, if they have shared it.
-   Allow aggregated of anonymized gameplay data from many users to be viewed by other users.
    Such as viewing the average time spent in a specific game area by players during their first playthrough.
-   Use your gameplay data to publish research papers and other publications, but only in an anonymized form.

## How do we store your data?

We securely store your data on servers located in the EU and the US. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

-   Your user data and gameplay metadata is stored in a database in the EU hosted by [PlanetScale](https://planetscale.com/).
-   Your gameplay data is stored in a database on a server in the EU hosted by [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/).
-   All personal data you provide is be processed and might partially be logged by vercel as a hosting provider.
    The progressing of your personal data may happen in the US under legal basis of the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) described in [GDPR article 46(2)(c) and (3)(a)](https://gdpr.eu/article-46-appropriate-safeguards-personal-data-transfers/).
    More information can be found in the [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
    and in the [Vercel Data Processing Addendum](https://vercel.com/legal/dpa).
-   If you choose to use the Discord login, your data is processed by Discord Inc. ("Discord").
    More information can be found in the [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).
-   If you choose to use the Google login, your data is processed by Google LLC ("Google").
    More information can be found in the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
-   All emails written to us and sent by us, are processed by Apple Inc. ("Apple").
    More information can be found in the [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/).

We will keep your data for as long as you have an account on our web application or mod.
If you choose to delete your account, contact us at <MailLink /> with your username or the email used to sign up.
We will get back to you to verify your account ownership and will delete all your data within 30 days.

## What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

**The right to access** – You have the right to request us for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

**The right to rectification** – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request us to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

**The right to erasure** – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

**The right to restrict processing** – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

**The right to object to processing** – You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

**The right to data portability** – You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: <MailLink />

## What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit [allaboutcookies.org](allaboutcookies.org).

## How do we use cookies?

We use cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our web application / mod, including:

-   Keeping you signed in
-   Remember your preferences

## What type of cookies do we use?

We only use functional cookies. These are cookies that are required for the operation of our web application / mod. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into our web application / mod and remember your preferences.

## How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our web application / mod features may not function as a result.

## Privacy policies of other websites

Our web application may contain links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our web application, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

## Changes to our privacy policy

We keep our privacy policy under regular review and place any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 18 December 2023.

## How to contact us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Email us at: <MailLink />
