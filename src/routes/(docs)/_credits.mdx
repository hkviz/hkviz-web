# About

The <HKVizText /> mod and this website is developed by me, Oliver. Initially as part of my bachelor's thesis. It allows to upload your gameplay data from
the game Hollow Knight and visualize it in various ways. You can contact me on Discord @OliverGrack or per mail <MailLinkUnstyled />.
