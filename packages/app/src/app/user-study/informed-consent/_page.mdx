## Goal of the research

We aim to explore how players engage with gameplay data visualizations in Metroidvania games, assess the perceived benefits, and identify the elements they consider most valuable.

Your participation in this study will contribute to the further development of this project
and to the advancement of knowledge on player-facing data visualization techniques and is greatly appreciated.

## Informed Consent Form: Please read carefully.

In this study, you will:

-   Fill a demographic questionnaire about you and your Hollow Knight playing habits.
-   In a call with me, you will:
    -   be asked to share your screen, no webcam video is required.
    -   Explore one gameplay using our visualizations.
    -   Complete a small number of tasks using the visualizations.
    -   Answer a few questions about your experience, and provide feedback.

All the information that we collect about you is kept strictly confidential. Confidentiality will be respected subject to legal constraints and professional guidelines.
Excerpts of your feedback, answers and exploration may appear in academic publications in anonymised form.
The session will be recorded and transcribed for analysis, the recording will be deleted after the study has been conducted.

You will have the opportunity to ask questions at the beginning and the end of the call, or by writing to Oliver Gstöttenbauer before or after the call (<MailLinkUnstyled /> or @olivergrack on Discord).
